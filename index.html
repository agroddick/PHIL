Using the headset

After purchase, follow the procedures to play music.

  • Putting the headset units into both ears

    Be sure to watch the video to put the headset units into both ears properly.

  • Establishing a BLUETOOTH® connection

    The Bluetooth function is turned on when both headset units are removed from the charging case.

    Launch the downloaded “Sony | Headphones Connect” app, then follow the on-screen instructions to establish a Bluetooth connection.


  • Operating the headset

    (1) Tap the headset unit or the areas around your ear where the headset is worn (wide area tap).

    (left/right): To play or pause music

    (left/right): To skip to the beginning of the next track



To use all functions and improved performances

Update the software of the headset and “Sony | Headphones Connect” app to the latest version. For details, refer to the following:

https://www.sony.net/elesupport/
